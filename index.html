VonEssenDesign

Sabina von Essen is an artist and engineer whose passion is technology and art. The combination of technology and art means that she has an unusual approach to creation. “I lack formal art education and I think this is a strength. I create my own rules and can allow myself to be youthful naive, ”says Sabina. Sabina performs independent and original paintings with acrylic paint. The motifs reflect emotions, situations and environments that affected the artist during her upbringing. The compositions can therefore be regarded as abstract interpretations of the artist's perspective of life. The works are innovative and modern and have been greatly appreciated. Sabina often works with gold and silver color to give the works a very unique effect, which makes them shimmer and become more vivid. With a strong faith and fascination with technology, her goal is to find the intersection of technology and art and thus a new digital way of perceiving art.
